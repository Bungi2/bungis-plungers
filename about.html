Our Story

Founded in 1974, Bungi's Plungers began with one simple mission: create reliable tools that homeowners and professionals could trust. What started as a small workshop has grown into a globally recognized manufacturer serving customers across more than 190 countries.

Through decades of innovation, quality craftsmanship, and an unreasonable commitment to defeating stubborn clogs, we have become one of the world's most respected names in plumbing equipment.
